★ Featured Work

Social Signal Pipeline

Power to Decide · Bedsider Provider Plus

Reproductive health misinformation doesn't wait for business hours. It spreads on TikTok and YouTube while your team is offline — and by the time someone flags a viral post, thousands of patients have already seen it. Social Signal was built to close that gap.

The system continuously scrapes TikTok and YouTube for SRH-related content using Apify agents, transcribes video audio with OpenAI Whisper, analyzes claims against trusted medical sources using a custom rubric-scoring model (0–14 scale), clusters trends semantically, and delivers a curated intelligence digest to Bedsider Provider Plus members every 1–2 weeks — with provider-ready talking points, viral post links, and suggested responses.

A hybrid human-AI review loop validates every flagged item before it reaches providers, maintaining accuracy and accountability at scale.

★ Thought Leadership

An AI Governance Roadmap
for Mission-Driven Leaders

Frame + Forge · Published Resource · Free to Share

Most AI governance guides are written for tech companies. This one is written for the ED who just found out a staff member has been pasting patient summaries into ChatGPT — and doesn't know what to do next. It's a 28-page governance guide for leaders who want clarity, confidence, and defensibility without a law degree or a technical team.

The roadmap introduces three original frameworks developed by Frame + Forge: the CLEAR Control Center (a five-gate decision framework for every AI use case), the AI Governance Readiness Gate (a pause-first screen for new adoption), and a 90-Day Visibility and Governance Plan that moves organizations from uncertainty to defensibility. It includes a board briefing summary designed to be extracted and handed directly to board members, an AI inventory worksheet, a vendor accountability checklist, and a glossary written for leaders, not engineers.

Designed to be shared across the organization — from frontline staff to the board chair. Because the more people who see the same picture, the faster governance takes hold.

The CLEAR Framework
C Clear Data Flow — Do we know what data enters this system, where it goes, and who can access it?
L Leadership-Owned Risk — Has leadership consciously reviewed this exposure — or inherited it by accident?
E Effort-Reducing Impact — Does this tool actually reduce burden, or create hidden monitoring and cleanup work?
A Articulable in Plain Language — Can we explain what this tool does to a board member, a funder, or the public?
R Responsibility Clearly Assigned — Who monitors outputs? Who has authority to pause or stop use?

SRH Reddit Monitoring for Data-Informed Education & Comms

Power to Decide · Bedsider · AbortionFinder

Real people asking real questions about reproductive health don't always do it in clinical settings or on official platforms. They do it on Reddit — anonymously, candidly, and at scale. This autonomous monitoring system tracked SRH-related Reddit communities continuously, surfacing what patients and the public were actually asking, worrying about, and getting wrong — in their own words, without requiring manual review or human-initiated searches.

Running without intervention, the system identified trending questions and concerns, flagged emerging misinformation themes, and delivered structured digests to the Bedsider, AbortionFinder, and Power to Decide teams on a regular cadence. Content strategists, educators, and communications leads received a direct, ongoing line to the real-world information needs of the communities they serve — allowing content strategy and educational material development to be shaped by what the data revealed, not assumptions about what audiences needed to hear.

Strategic Design · Prototype

Autonomous Title X Clinic Database Updater

National SRH Organization (Anonymous)

Title X clinic directories go stale constantly — and a patient who follows bad data to a clinic that no longer provides Title X-funded services faces real consequences for their care and access.

This prototype monitors OPA and Title X directories, verifying 48 service, access, and funding data points per clinic against official sources only. Potential changes are flagged for human review via email alert — nothing touches the live database without approval. Full audit trail at every step.
